# Mastercard Foundation — Application & Partnership Analysis

**Status:** ✅ ACTIVE FOR ADUGBOINSURE  
**URL:** https://mastercardfdn.org/en/

## How They Work

**No direct grants.** Instead:
- MCF funds through **partner organizations** (1,100+ programs across Africa + Canada)
- MCF provides grants to NGOs, social enterprises, and institutions to run programs
- Organizations wanting MCF support must either:
  1. Become a partner organization (win MCF contract)
  2. Join existing MCF partner programs as a beneficiary

## Key Relevant Programs for AdugboInsure

**Focus Area: Health** (directly relevant)
- MCF explicitly funds health programs (see filters on partners page)
- Limited health-specific programs listed, but searchable

**Geographic Focus:**
- Nigeria + 40 countries in Africa
- Offices in Lagos, Accra, Nairobi, Kigali, Kampala, Dakar, Addis Ababa
- Perfect coverage for AdugboInsure

**Youth Focus:**
- Target: 30M young people access dignified work by 2030
- Overlap: Health insurance = dignified work pathway for community health agents

## How to Apply

### Option 1: Become a Direct Partner (Strategic)
**Target:** Mastercard Foundation invites strategic partner organizations  
**Process:**
- Contact MCF directly: Contact Us link → https://mastercardfdn.org/en/contact-us/
- Demonstrate track record in health + youth employment
- Pitch AdugboInsure as aligned with MCF goals
- MCF may fund AdugboInsure as a partner organization

### Option 2: Join Existing Partner Programs (Faster)
**Identify relevant MCF partner programs:**
- Filter partners by:
  - Country: Nigeria
  - Focus Area: Health (or Education/Digital as adjacent)
- Contact individual partners → ask about participation

### Option 3: Vendor/Supplier Relationship
**Check:** "Vendor Policies and Guidelines" (link available on site)
- MCF may contract vendors for services
