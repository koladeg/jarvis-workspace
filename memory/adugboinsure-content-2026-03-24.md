## 🏥 ADUGBOINSURE AGENT TRAINING GUIDE
### Understanding Community-Based Health Insurance (CBHI)

---

### PART 1 — WHAT IS COMMUNITY HEALTH INSURANCE?

Community-Based Health Insurance (CBHI) — what we call *AdugboInsure* — is a health coverage plan where people in the same community come together, contribute small amounts regularly, and help each other pay for medical bills when someone falls sick.

Think of it like a *community savings group (ajo/esusu)* — but instead of saving for goods or business, you're saving for health. Nobody plans to get sick, but when it happens, the community fund is there to cover it.

**Key idea:** *Many small contributions = one big safety net.*

---

### PART 2 — WHY IT MATTERS (THE PROBLEM IT SOLVES)

In Nigeria today:
- Over 70% of people pay for healthcare from their own pocket (out-of-pocket)
- One serious illness can wipe out a family's savings
- Many people delay going to the hospital because they fear the cost
- Preventable deaths happen simply because people can't afford treatment

**AdugboInsure breaks this cycle.**

When someone is enrolled, they don't have to panic about hospital bills. They already have coverage — paid for gradually, affordably, as a community.

---

### PART 3 — HOW IT WORKS (SIMPLE STEPS)

1. **Enroll** — The agent registers community members and collects their basic info
2. **Contribute** — Members pay a small monthly or weekly premium (e.g., ₦500–₦2,000/month)
3. **Pool** — All contributions are pooled into a community health fund
4. **Claim** — When a member gets sick, they go to a partner clinic/hospital and show their membership card
5. **Cover** — The fund pays the hospital directly for covered services (consultations, drugs, tests, deliveries, etc.)
6. **Renew** — Members renew every quarter or annually

---

### PART 4 — YOUR ROLE AS A POS AGENT

You are the most important link in this system. Here's what you do:

✅ **Enroll new members** — Explain the plan, register them on the app/platform, collect premium payments

✅ **Collect premiums** — Accept cash or transfer, issue receipts, remit to the central fund

✅ **Educate your community** — Help people understand what is covered and how to use it

✅ **Support claims** — Help members know which hospitals are partners and how to present their card

✅ **Build trust** — Your reputation is the product. When people trust you, they enroll.

**You earn a commission for every enrollment and renewal.**

### PART 5 — COMMON QUESTIONS FROM COMMUNITY MEMBERS

**Q: What if I don't fall sick — do I lose my money?**
A: Your contribution protects the whole community. If you don't use it this period, you're still covered next time. Think of it like a fire extinguisher — you hope you never need it, but you're glad it's there.

**Q: What does the plan cover?**
A: Typically: outpatient consultations, lab tests, medications, maternity care, and minor procedures. Serious surgeries may have a co-pay. Always check your plan document.

**Q: Can I join anytime?**
A: Yes, but there is usually a waiting period (e.g., 30 days) before you can make claims after enrollment.

**Q: What if the hospital is not on the list?**
A: Always go to a partner facility for coverage. Emergency cases have special provisions.

**Q: What about my children?**
A: Children under 5 can often be enrolled at a discounted or free rate. Ask your agent.

### PART 6 — KEY SELLING POINTS FOR AGENTS

When talking to community members, emphasize:

🔹 **Affordable** — Less than ₦50 per day in most plans
🔹 **Accessible** — No complicated paperwork; enroll with your agent today
🔹 **Community-owned** — Your community controls and benefits from the fund
🔹 **No discrimination** — Old or young, sick or healthy — everyone can join
🔹 **Immediate peace of mind** — Stop worrying about "what if I get sick"
