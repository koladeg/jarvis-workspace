# Referee Email Template

## Subject options
- MBA Recommendation Request — Needed by May
- Request for MBA Reference Letter
- Could you support my MBA applications with a recommendation?

---

## Email template

Dear [Referee Name],

I hope you are doing well.

I’m preparing my MBA applications for the 2026 intake, and I wanted to ask if you would be willing to support me with a **professional recommendation letter**.

I am applying to a shortlist of business schools in **Canada and Australia**, with the goal of starting my MBA in **September 2026**. My broader plan is to strengthen my leadership, strategy, and entrepreneurial skills through the MBA, gain post-study work experience, and eventually build a startup in the USA.

Because you have directly seen my work and professional growth, I believe your recommendation would carry real weight.

### What I would need from you
- A recommendation/reference submitted through each school’s online portal
- Main deadline target: **by May 2026**
- I will send you:
  - my updated CV/résumé,
  - a short summary of my career goals,
  - the schools I am applying to,
  - key projects/achievements you may wish to mention.

### Points you may highlight if helpful
- My leadership potential
- My problem-solving and initiative
- My communication and teamwork
- My growth trajectory and readiness for graduate business study
- Any examples of ownership, resilience, or innovation

If you are comfortable supporting me, I would be very grateful. Please let me know and I will send over the full application pack right away.

Thank you very much for considering this.

Kind regards,  
**Kolade**
