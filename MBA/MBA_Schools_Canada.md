# MBA Schools — Canada

Focus: programs that best support **MBA → post-study work → North America network → eventual USA startup path**.

> Verify every deadline directly on the school website before applying. Intake structures and application rounds can change yearly.

---

## 1) Rotman School of Management — University of Toronto
- **Location:** Toronto
- **Program:** Full-Time MBA
- **Why it fits:** Top brand, strongest Toronto ecosystem, good for consulting/finance/product/startups.
- **Admission basics:** degree, work experience, essays/interview, references, GMAT/GRE accepted.
- **GMAT target for safety:** **700+ ideal**; competitive profile usually matters more than score alone.
- **Known deadline info captured:** 2026 cycle page listed R1 **Sep 10 2025**, R2 **Nov 19 2025**, R3 **Mar 4 2026**, R4 **May 13 2026** (domestic only).
- **Official page:** https://www.rotman.utoronto.ca/programs/mba-programs/full-time-mba/
- **Research angle:** entrepreneurship in Toronto, CDL ecosystem, US proximity.

## 2) Ivey Business School — Western University
- **Location:** London, Ontario
- **Program:** MBA
- **Why it fits:** Strong case-method teaching, leadership brand, good Canadian employer reputation.
- **Admission basics:** degree, work experience, references, essays/interview, GMAT/GRE.
- **Known deadline info captured:** rolling admissions; 2027 intake page showed **Apr 13 2026**, **Jul 6 2026**, **Sep 4 2026**, **Nov 2 2026**; July listed as final international round.
- **Official page:** https://www.ivey.uwo.ca/mba/admissions/
- **Research angle:** leadership training and network depth across Canada.

## 3) McGill Desautels
- **Location:** Montreal
- **Program:** MBA
- **Why it fits:** International brand, bilingual city, strong innovation/startup scene.
- **Admission basics:** minimum **2 years** work experience, **2 references**, transcripts, GMAT/GRE required, interview by invitation, English test if needed.
- **Known deadline info captured for Fall 2026:** **Nov 15 2025**, **Jan 15 2026**, **Mar 15 2026** (final international), **May 15 2026** (final citizens/PR).
- **Essay prompts already published:**
  1. Why MBA now and what are your goals?
  2. What qualities matter in a classmate and how will you contribute?
  3. Describe a difficult decision.
- **Official page:** https://www.mcgill.ca/desautels/programs/mba-programs/mba/admissions
- **Research angle:** Montreal affordability relative to Toronto/Vancouver and strong AI/startup energy.

## 4) UBC Sauder
- **Location:** Vancouver
- **Program:** MBA
- **Why it fits:** Pacific gateway, startup and tech scene, excellent city for innovation.
- **Admission basics:** degree, work experience, references, essays/video/interview may apply, GMAT/GRE typically considered.
- **Deadline note:** verify exact rounds on current admissions page.
- **Official page:** https://www.sauder.ubc.ca/programs/mba/admissions
- **Research angle:** Vancouver startup network + west coast proximity.

## 5) Queen’s Smith School of Business
- **Location:** Kingston
- **Program:** MBA
- **Why it fits:** strong leadership focus and reputable Canadian brand.
- **Admission basics:** degree, work experience, references, interview, GMAT/GRE usually considered.
- **Deadline note:** verify intake timing and international timing directly.
- **Official page:** https://smith.queensu.ca/mba_programs/mba/admission/index.php
- **Research angle:** strong alumni base and leadership development.

## 6) Schulich School of Business — York University
- **Location:** Toronto
- **Program:** MBA
- **Why it fits:** flexible specialization options, Toronto location, broad industry exposure.
- **Admission basics:** degree, essays, references, work experience, GMAT/GRE depending on profile/pathway.
- **Deadline note:** verify latest deadlines and intake windows directly.
- **Official page:** https://schulich.yorku.ca/programs/mba/
- **Research angle:** value-for-money relative to other Toronto options.

## Application Notes to Prepare for Canada
- Keep résumé achievement-focused and quantified.
- Have 2 professional referees ready.
- Explain why MBA now, why entrepreneurship later, and why Canada is the best bridge to a US startup path.
- Be ready to show funding capacity and realistic settlement planning.
