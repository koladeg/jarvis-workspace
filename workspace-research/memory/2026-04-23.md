## Pipeline-level weak spots and blockers

### 1) Scheduler activity is outrunning real research activity
- The scheduler is functioning, but many due runs only append honest placeholders.
- This preserves cadence visibility, but it does not materially improve lane outputs.

### 2) Delivery/reporting is still noisy
- `send-research-rollout-update.sh` can still send a research update based on the latest note even when that note is mostly scheduled markers.
- `logs/research-rollout.log` shows repeated sends, and `logs/research-activity.log` is dominated by `No new research activity`.
- This is better than fake claims, but still noisy enough to dilute attention.

### 3) Duplicate schedule logging suggests cron duplication or repeated triggering
- `logs/research-rollout-schedule.log` shows many duplicated lines for the same timestamps.
- That should be treated as an operational bug until proven intentional.

### 4) The mistake-review loop is too generic
- It runs often, but the content is repetitive.
- It is not yet producing concrete lane-specific fixes such as pruning dead sources, tightening output schemas, or proving sub-agent parallel review.
