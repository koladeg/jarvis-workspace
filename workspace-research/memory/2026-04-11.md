- **Configuration mismatch:** the cheapest live trucks tend to be tractor heads, non-flatbeds, or other body styles.
- **Age drift:** near-budget body-style matches are usually older than the target window.
- **HOWO availability remains weak in the EU used-market lane.**

If the budget remains around **£4,000**, the likely outcomes are still:
- wrong-body vehicles,
- older trucks outside the preferred age band,
- rough-condition/project trucks,
- or listings that are only partially aligned with the spec.

The truck lane likely improves only if at least one constraint moves: **budget up**, **body tolerance wider**, **brand tolerance wider**, or **age/condition tolerance looser**.
