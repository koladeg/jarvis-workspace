### Why there is still no fit
- **Budget mismatch remains the main blocker.** Prior validated pass (2026-03-24) already showed the market reality: DAF / IVECO 4x2 flatbed trucks in the desired power band generally price far above the working budget.
- **Configuration mismatch is the second blocker.** Cheap trucks that do appear tend to be the wrong body type (tractor units, non-flatbeds, other body configs) or not in the preferred brand/power window.
- **Retention problem:** older links do not reliably stay live, so “saved candidates” decay fast unless they are actively refreshed.

### Practical note
If the budget is still around **£4,000**, the search is likely to keep returning wrong-body vehicles, project trucks, very old units, or dead listings. To get meaningful candidates faster, the search will probably need one of these constraints relaxed:
- raise budget,
- accept weaker brands / lower power,
- widen body-type tolerance,
- or widen age / condition tolerance.
