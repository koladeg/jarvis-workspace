## 📋 COMPANY OVERVIEW

### Company Name
**AdugboInsure**

### Tagline
*"Community-Powered Health Insurance for Underserved Nigeria"*

### One-Line Pitch
AdugboInsure is a digital health insurance platform enabling State Health Insurance Agencies and community health workers to enroll, manage, and serve underserved populations in Nigeria through affordable, accessible community-based insurance schemes.

### Mission
To empower community health agents (POS operators, traditional healers, pharmacists, traders) as digital insurance agents, enabling sustainable health coverage for underserved populations in Nigeria.

---

## 🎯 THE PROBLEM

### Market Opportunity
- **Population:** 200M+ in Nigeria
- **Uninsured:** 80%+ lack formal health insurance
- **Barrier:** Cost, complexity, lack of trust in digital systems
- **Current Gap:** SHIA (State Health Insurance Agencies) struggle to reach communities due to:
  - Lack of last-mile agents
  - Complex enrollment processes
  - Low digital literacy in communities
  - No trusted local touchpoints

### Why This Matters
- Families delay medical care due to cost → complications worsen → costs rise exponentially
- Small traders/farmers (40M+ in Nigeria) are excluded from formal insurance
- Community health workers are underutilized as channel partners
- **Market size:** ₦50B+ annual health insurance opportunity in Nigeria alone

---

## 💡 THE SOLUTION

### AdugboInsure Platform
**A digital marketplace connecting:**
1. **State Health Insurance Agencies** (supply-side)
2. **Community Health Workers** (distribution channel)
3. **Underserved Populations** (demand-side)

### Key Features
✅ **One-click enrollment** — Agents sign up families via WhatsApp/SMS  
✅ **Flexible payment plans** — ₦3,375/quarter (₦45/day) down to ₦15,000/year  
✅ **Community-owned model** — Fund balance transparency, agent incentives  
✅ **Smart coverage** — Outpatient, maternity, emergency, meds, labs, surgeries  
✅ **Performance incentives** — Agents earn ₦500-₦1,000 per successful enrollment  
✅ **No exclusions** — Covers healthy and sick, young and old, traders and farmers  

### Technology Stack
- **Mobile-first:** WhatsApp API + SMS for low-literacy enrollment
- **Backend:** Digital enrollment system + agent dashboard
- **Payment:** Paystack + Moniepoint for distributed collections
- **Analytics:** Real-time enrollment tracking + agent performance metrics
