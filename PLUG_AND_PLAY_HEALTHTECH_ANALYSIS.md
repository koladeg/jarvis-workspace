## Cohort Structure

**Typical Plug & Play Cohort:**
- **Duration:** 3-4 months intensive
- **Format:** Weekly mentor sessions, investor pitches, demo days
- **Funding:** $50K-$500K+ (varies by program)
- **Equity:** Typically **0-10% equity take** (non-dilutive option available)
- **Mentors:** Corporate partners + industry experts
- **Demo Day:** Pitch to 100+ investors
